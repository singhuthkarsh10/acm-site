EVENT 1

I ACUMEN is an Intra-Department technical meet for MCAs.The events mainly center around the first year students of the MCA family and aim to train them in both technical and managerial abilities.

An MCA exclusive meet, conducted by ACM, brings in the freshers to prove their might in the two-day meet in the month of August-September. Software Presentation, Coding, Dumb Charades, Quiz, Marketing, Web Designing, Surprise, Oratorio, Essay Writing and many other events define ACUMEN.

The three batches of MCA circumambulate around the spinning wheel of ACUMEN vying to snatch every spoke.
